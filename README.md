# Steer Cam

A completely customizable driver camera mod for BeamNG.drive with steering input to pan cockpit view.

## Main Features
- **Fully customizable**: Tweak EVERYTHING!* From basic tweaks like camera position, tilt and pan within the cockpit,
  to custom glance camera angles and cool immersive extras! All the controls are made available to you,
  so you can create your own perfectly tailored camera experience. 
- **Camera pan on turning**: The titular feature. Change the view to look in the direction
  you are turning! Works with steering wheels, keyboard and controller.
- **Make or change presets**: Play around with all the different settings to create your ideal camera setup. Then, share 
  it with your friends! Oh, and did I mention you can customize your setup **Per Vehicle Type**? Have the massive Gavril T
  overlook the road whilst the tiny Piccolino puts your view front and center, ready for the race.
- **Blind-spot glance**: Use your camera as you would your head! Practice paralell parking, get that authentic city drive  
  experience and keep track of your surroundings. Adds customizable hotkeys in Options → Controls → Bindings (Camera). Toggle or hold.
- **Immersive extras** A subcategory of options with subtle (if that tickles your fancy) camera effects. 
  Have the camera change FOV at speed. Feel the vibration of the engine as it rumbles on ignition, and 
  the subtle rumble of the road as you push your car to it's limits. Check out Dev's Preset (my personal
  preferences) for a subtle and immersive experience, that lets you feel the texture of the road,
  the physics of your car, and immerses you in the driving experience without being distracting.

_*not actually everything... but pretty close!_

The name SteerCam comes from the first feature I implemented which is effectively just panning the camera
based on steer input, but the project has grown a lot in scope since that initial version.

## Installation
**Option A:** Install via ingame repository browser (Recommended). Search for "SteerCam" made by the user "LarsGudm"

**Option B:** Manual installation:
1. Download the latest `steercam_larsgudm.zip` from Releases.
2. Drop it into your BeamNG `mods` folder (Launcher → Manage User Folder → Open User Folder). Leave it zipped.
3. Launch Beam.NG
4. Options → Camera → Switching order → tick **Steercam**, then press `C` to reach it.
5. Add the **Steer Cam Settings** app from Esc → UI Apps → Add App to tune it.

## Compatibility
- BeamNG.drive 0.38+.
- Adds a new camera type rather than overwriting one, so should work with other camera mods!
